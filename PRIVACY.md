# Privacy Policy for ScubaFlow

**Effective Date:** June 20, 2026

Tomaso Bulligan ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ScubaFlow web application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you play our game.

## 1. 100% Client-Side & Local Processing

ScubaFlow is designed with privacy in mind. **We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.** 

* **Local Music Processing:** When you upload an audio track to play, the file is read and processed **entirely within your web browser** using the Web Audio API. Your audio data is never uploaded, stored, or sent to any server.
* **No User Accounts:** You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details to use ScubaFlow.
* **No Tracking or Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics, tracking cookies, or advertising networks.

## 2. Hosting and Server Logs

ScubaFlow is hosted statically using GitHub Pages. When you access the website, standard web server logs (including your IP address, browser type, and time of access) are collected and processed by GitHub as the hosting provider. You can refer to the [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) for details on how GitHub handles hosting traffic.

## 3. Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect any personal identifiable information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
