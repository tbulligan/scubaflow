ScubaFlow

Neon Buoyancy Music Game

HOW TO PLAY:

⬆️ Spacebar (HOLD): Increase buoyancy to rise up.

⬇️ Spacebar (RELEASE): Decrease buoyancy to sink down.

💎 Collectibles: Align your path precisely with the neon debris. Auto-collect is disabled; gathering is a pure test of your buoyancy skills!

🌫️ Avoid Silt-Outs: Colliding with the cave walls triggers a silt-out, blinding you and resetting your flow multiplier. Stay centered to maintain your flow!

Autopilot (Music Visualizer Mode)
Mutes game sounds to focus purely on your music track
